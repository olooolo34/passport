How to GEt Your Passport

To be eligible for a passport, applicants must first meet certain documentation requirements. These include possessing a valid national ID, a recent passport-sized photograph, and an official birth certificate. Only individuals who have all of these documents are considered eligible to apply for and receive a passport.
